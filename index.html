How It Works

Get Started in 30 Seconds

No complicated setup. Install, add your words, and browse normally.

1

Install the Extension

One click from the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, or Edge Add-ons. No account required, no data collection.

2

Add Your Words

Open the options page, click "Add Rule," and type what to find and what to replace it with.

3

Browse Normally

Words replace automatically on every page. Toggle the extension on/off anytime from the popup.

4

Share Your List

Generate a share link and send it to friends. They import your rules instantly.
