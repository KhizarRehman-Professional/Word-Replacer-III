Word Replacer III is and always will be completely free. If you want to support the work, here is how.

I didn't create it entirely; it is a continuation of Word Replacer II made by G3Kappa. I just added more features, edited some things, upgraded the manifest to v3, added a new UI, and so on.

After the discontinuation of Word Replacer II by Chrome due to a manifest issue, I would have had to switch to Firefox. There was a word replacer there, but in uni — a mf bullshit uni — they used Chrome or Edge. Getting permission to install Firefox or other things was restricted, so I extracted the file from the extension source and would manually load it into my extensions. But, but, but, as expected of my bullshit uni, they restricted Edge too. These mfs will do anything but improve other things at the uni. So well, yeah, I decided to just upgrade the manifest to v3 for myself.

I had no plan to have it in the Chrome extension store, but then I got hooked on this extension — updating, editing, or whatever you say. Plus, I am currently learning web development while I am doing this, so yeah, it could also help me understand it. Yeah, blah blah blah.

According to G3Kappa, this extension was meant to be free, so yeah, I respect it, and it will also be completely free from my end too. It will never be premium, but if you still want to donate to me, you can. But I cannot guarantee that I will keep updating or upgrading it; I might altogether even disappear lmao, as always, like the failure I am. But anyways, Thank you for using Word Replacer III!
